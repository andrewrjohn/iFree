What is iFree?

The iFree movement is simple: downgrade your phone, upgrade your life. iFree is dedicated to restoring the more simple pleasures of life while improving your lifestyle.

"Smartphone Not Included"

Smartphones are popular (duh). Studies show that almost two-thirds of Americans own a smartphone, that's over 210 million people in the US alone! A study taken in 2015 also showed that 15% of Americans ages 18-29 are heavily dependent on a smartphone for online access. Out of all those smartphone users, a little over half of them frequently or occasionally reach the maximum data allowed on their smartphone plan. Not only are a lot of people using smartphones, but they're on them a lot.
